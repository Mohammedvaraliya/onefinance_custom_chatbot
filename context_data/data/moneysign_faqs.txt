What are 5 personality traits?
O-Openness to Experience, C-Conscientiousness, Control(self-discipline, Consistency, reliability), E-Extra version, A-Agreeblness (trust, cooperation), N-Nervousness (Neuroticism-feelings) fearness, anxiety.

What is OCEAN model?
It is a framework that helps you understand your psychology towards your finance.

Who built the OCEAN model?
Originally developed in 1949, the big 5 personality traits is a theory established by D. W. Fiske and later expanded upon by other researchers including Norman (1967), Smith (1967), Goldberg (1981), and McCrae & Costa (1987).
